MASTERCARD INTERNATIONAL INC., Purchase, N.Y., the card company, bought the TowerGroup, Needham, Mass., a financial services research operation, from the Reuters Group, London, for an undisclosed sum to win clients for its MasterCard Advisers unit.
CENTER FINANCIAL CORP., Los Angeles, an owner of banks in Southern California, said it planned to buy Liberty Bank of New York for undisclosed terms.
WEYERHAEUSER CO., Federal Way, Wash., an owner and manager of commercial forest land, said it planned to sell about 300,000 acres of forest in Georgia, the latest in a series of sales to reduce debt since it acquired Willamette Industries.
