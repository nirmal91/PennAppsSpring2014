India's largest consumer products company, Hindustan Lever Ltd., said its profit rose 3 percent, to 5 billion rupees ($110 million), in the fourth quarter. The company, which makes Surf detergent and Pepsodent toothpaste among other products, said overall sales were slightly lower but rose 2.2 percent when discontinued brands were excluded. The results fell short of expectations, and shares in Hindustan Lever fell 7 percent. The chairman, M.S. Banga, said that he expected sales to increase this year.
