TiVo in San Jose, Calif., has named Grant, Scott &amp; Hurley in San Francisco as its agency of record after more than a year without one. Advertising spending is expected to increase to $10 million to $12 million a year from an estimated $1 million last year as TiVo seeks to stimulate growth for its personal video recorders. The decision was made after a review. The other finalists were not identified.
