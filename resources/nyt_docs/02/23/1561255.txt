Blackfriars Communications, Maynard, Mass., in its first edition of the Blackfriars Marketing Index, a quarterly report intended to measure corporate marketing demand and spending, set the index at 119 for the first quarter. That means the executives responding to a Blackfriars survey said they expected to spend 19 percent more on marketing in the quarter than they spent on average in each quarter of 2003.
