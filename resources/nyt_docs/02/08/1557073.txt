I wonder, would our police treat this slavery more seriously if the slaves were being recruited for, say, domestic labor? Eliot Spitzer, the attorney general of New York, campaigned aggressively for wages owed to underpaid immigrant workers. But because these girls are slaves in an illegal, despicable activity, it is easier for the powers-that-be to pretend it doesn't exist.
Joanne Kennedy  Monsey, N.Y.
