Flowers or sweets for Valentine's Day? Lilyfield Cakes, a company in Winnipeg, Canada, sells them together. The company's tube cakes come with a bouquet of fresh flowers in the center. The cakes, which serve about 10, are packed in satin boxes decorated with silk flowers. The chocolate cake, left, is brownielike in flavor and texture; there are also vanilla and coconut cakes. For Valentine's Day, the cake shown here is $140. Choices featuring other flowers range from $80 to $225 plus shipping. Orders must be placed 48 hours in advance for delivery on Tuesdays through Fridays, from (866) 832-0884 or www.lilyfieldcakes.com.
