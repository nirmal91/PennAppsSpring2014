The Department of Defense has identified 526 American service members who have died since the start of the Iraq war. It confirmed the death of the following Americans yesterday:
SORIANO, Armando, 20, Pvt. First Class, Army; Houston; Third Squadron, Third Armored Cavalry.
TURNER, Roger C. Jr., 37, Staff Sgt., Army; Parkersburg, W.Va.; Fourth Infantry Division.
