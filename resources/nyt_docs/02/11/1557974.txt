The Department of Defense has identified 533 American service members who have died since the start of the Iraq war. It confirmed the deaths of the following Americans yesterday:
BUNDA, Christopher, 29, Staff Sgt., Army; Washington; Second Battalion, Third Infantry.
RAMEY, Richard P., 27, Staff Sgt., Army; Canton, Ohio; 703rd Ordnance Company, supporting the 82nd Airborne Division.
