India's gross domestic product is estimated to grow at a rate of 8.1 percent in the fiscal year ending in March 2004, making India's one of the fastest-growing economies in the world. Advance data released by the government's Central Statistical Organization said growth would be helped by the farm sector, which was expected to grow by 9.1 percent, compared with a drop of 5.2 percent a year ago. Manufacturing is expected to grow 7.1 percent compared with 6.2 percent a year ago. India's economic output grew 4 percent in the year that ended in March 2003.
