McGEOGH, Holly J., 19, Pvt. First Class, Army; Taylor, Mich.; Fourth Infantry Division.
MIERSANDOVAL, Eliu A., 27, Sgt., Army; San Clemente, Calif.; Fourth Infantry Division.
MORENO, Luis A., 19; Pvt. First Class, Army; New York City; Battery A, Fourth Battalion, First Field Artillery.
