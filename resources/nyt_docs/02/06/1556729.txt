Ned Russell was named executive vice president at the New York office of Arnold Worldwide, part of the Arnold Worldwide Partners division of Havas. He succeeds John Tracosas, who left to become president of the Irvine, Calif., office of Foote, Cone &amp; Belding, part of the Interpublic Group of Companies. Mr. Russell had been chief operating officer at the New York office of the Wolf Group, owned by Wolf Group Integrated Communications.
Walter Petersen was named to a new post, executive vice president and chief communications officer, at the Partnership, responsible for the Draft and Lowe &amp; Partners Worldwide units. He had been a consultant to the Partnership, part of the Interpublic Group of Companies, and before that held senior communications posts at the Bcom3 Group, Chicago, now part of the Publicis Groupe.
