JAZZ FOR FAMILIES, at two Manhattan locations on Sunday. At 1 p.m., Hayes Greenfield's ''Jazz-a-Ma-Tazz,'' featuring the jazz saxophonist, will be at Porters, 216 Seventh Avenue, at 22nd Street, Chelsea. Adults, $5; free for children; reservations: (212) 229-2878. At 2 p.m., the Jazz Standard Youth Orchestra will perform and answer questions at the club, 116 East 27th Street; donation, $5; information: (212) 576-2232.
''WHAT IS ORCHESTRATION?'' Part of the New York Philharmonic's ''Young People's Concert'' series, with music by John Corigliano, who will also appear as host, tomorrow at 2 p.m. at Avery Fisher Hall. Tickets, $5 to $25. Information: (212) 875-5656.
SOUL ON ICE, an ice skating party in Central Park, at the Lasker Ice Rink, at 110th Street, Harlem, tomorrow from 12:30 to 3 p.m. Admission, $10, includes skate rental, food and a raffle; proceeds benefit Figure Skating in Harlem, the event's sponsor. Information: (646) 698-3440.

''EVERYONE MAKES MUSIC,'' the musical production of ''Sesame Street Live,'' through Feb. 16 at the Theater at Madison Square Garden, Seventh Avenue and 31st Street, Manhattan, (212) 465-6741. Today at 11 a.m. and 2 p.m.; tomorrow and Sunday at 10:30 a.m. and 2 and 5:30 p.m. Tickets, $15 to $52.50.
WATCHMAGIC, magic and comedy with Oz Pearlman, Saturdays at 5 p.m. through March 27 at the Joyous Life Theater, 119 West 23rd Street, Chelsea, (212) 352-3101. Tickets, $35.
''DESERT TRAVELS,'' tales from the Middle East, told with music and dance by the Treehouse Shakers, at two locations. Tomorrow at 3 p.m. at the Broome Corner Performance Space, 425 Broome Street, No. 4R, SoHo. Tickets: $12; $10 for children; information, (212) 715-1914. Also Sunday at 2 p.m. at the Brooklyn Arts Exchange, 421 Fifth Avenue, at Eighth Street, Park Slope, (718) 832-0018. Tickets: $10; $8 for children.
TU B'SHEVAT CRAFT WORKSHOP mixes nature with Jewish traditions, Sunday at 2:30 p.m. at the Museum of Jewish Heritage, 36 Battery Place, Lower Manhattan, (212) 945-0039. Included in museum admission: $10; members and 55+, $7; students and under 12, $5; holders of family memberships, free.
THE KANGAROO GARDEN will perform songs tomorrow at 11 a.m. at the Riverside Church, 91 Claremont Avenue, Morningside Heights, (212) 870-6722. Donation: $5; $2 for children.
''HORSING AROUND,'' activities based on Native American cowboys, tomorrow, 1 to 4 p.m., at the Smithsonian National Museum of the American Indian, 1 Bowling Green, Lower Manhattan. Free. Information: (212) 514-3888.
