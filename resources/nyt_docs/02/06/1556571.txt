FOR some, the bright cold sun of winter awakens a desire for outdoor fun. For others, the antidote to winter's chill is an overstuffed chair. For those who belong to the latter category, a few hours spent knitting can be the perfect way to relax on a weekend afternoon. Whether you're new to knitting or an expert, there are plenty of products to keep your fingers dancing until spring.
BETHANY LYTTLE
Correction:  March 5, 2004, Friday  The Shopping List column on Feb. 6, about knitting supplies, referred incorrectly to the Clover Thread Cutter Pendant, a device for clipping yarn. Though it is intended to be worn as a pendant, it does not come on a cord.
