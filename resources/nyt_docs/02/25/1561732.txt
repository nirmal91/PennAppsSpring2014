Camden High School of New Jersey became the first United States schoolboy 4x200 meter relay to run under 1 minute 27 seconds when the foursome of Dwayne Lewis (21.9 seconds), Sherron Bullock (21.3), Alonzo Brown (21.9) and Carl Smith 21.8 won the event last night in 1:26.90 in the Eastern States high school championships at the Armory Track and Field Center in Manhattan.
