The Rev. Patrick G. Duggan has been chosen by Nassau County's executive, Thomas R. Suozzi, as his new deputy for economic development. Mr. Duggan, whose appointment is to be officially announced today, replaces Peter Sylver, who resigned several weeks ago over questions of fiscal irregularities. Mr. Duggan is the pastor of the Congregational Church of South Hempstead/United Church of Christ and is the executive director of Sustainable Long Island, a nonprofit group that advocates economic growth that protects the environment and meets social needs like housing.
Bruce Lambert (NYT)
