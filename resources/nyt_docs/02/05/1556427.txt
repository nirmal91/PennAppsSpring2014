Radio advertising revenue rose 1 percent in 2003 compared with 2002, the Radio Advertising Bureau in New York reported, as the year ended with a slight increase.
For the year, the 1 percent gain came as local ad revenue showed no increase but national ad revenue rose 6 percent.
For December, combined ad revenue rose 2 percent, as the local and national categories both rose 2 percent.
The increase in December came after a 1 percent decline in October and a 4 percent decline in November compared with those months in 2002.
