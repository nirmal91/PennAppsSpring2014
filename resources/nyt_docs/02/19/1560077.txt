To the Editor:
Re ''Amazon Glitch Unmasks War of Reviewers'' (front page, Feb. 14):
I'm a mystery author who is puzzled by a mystery. Why should the identity of any reviewer, in any format, be hidden? Shouldn't a reviewer be willing to be identified as the author of a review? If not, why should I bother to read it?
GAIL FARRELLY  Bronxville, N.Y., Feb. 14, 2004
