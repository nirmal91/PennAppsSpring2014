Also making its Paris debut, a brunch set from the London designer Jasper Morrison, who makes uncluttered designs for Alessi, Magis and Vitra. The brunch set has a toaster, an electric kettle and a clever coffee maker (right) with useful slots (top) for filters and a measuring spoon. ''I allowed the function to dictate the shape,'' Mr. Morrison said. Now at select Habitat stores in Paris (the coffee maker sells for about $190); it is expected to be available this summer in the United States.
