Because of an editing error, an article yesterday about a column on the Op-Ed page of The New York Times by the United Auto Workers and the Sierra Club, opposing a plan by the Bush administration to overhaul fuel economy rules, omitted comment from the government. Rae Tyson, a spokesman for the National Highway Traffic Safety Administration, said that the proposals were at an early stage and that suggestions are being sought from all parties, including the union and environmental groups.
