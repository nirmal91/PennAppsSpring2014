MTV Networks, New York, part of Viacom, has changed the name of CTN: College Television Network, which it acquired in 2002, to mtvU. Stephen Friedman continues as general manager at the renamed cable network, which is carried by more than 720 colleges and universities.
Ad spending for 2004 is projected to increase 7.8 percent from 2003, to $138.4 billion, according to a forecast from TNS Media Intelligence/CMR, New York, part of Taylor Nelson Sofres. Estimated ad spending in 2003 rose 6.7 percent from 2002, to $128.4 billion, the company said.
