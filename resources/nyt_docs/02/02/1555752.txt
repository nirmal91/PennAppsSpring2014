The American Advertising Federation in Washington has named four executives to join the Advertising Hall of Fame for 2004.
The honorees are Karl Eller, former chairman and chief executive of the Clear Channel Outdoor division of Clear Channel Communications; Philip H. Geier Jr., former chairman and chief executive at the Interpublic Group of Companies; John E. Pepper, former chairman and chief executive at Procter &amp; Gamble; and Liener Temerlin, chairman emeritus at Temerlin McClain Advertising. The executives will be honored at a lunch on March 16 in New York.
