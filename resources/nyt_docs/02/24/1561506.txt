For the second time in three days, Natasha Hastings, a senior at A. P. Randolph High School, set a national best for this indoor season at the Armory Track and Field Center. Her winning time last night was 38.16 seconds in the girls' 300-meter dash at the Public Schools Athletic League Manhattan Borough championships. Hastings won the 400 meters in 54.14 seconds at a high school invitational meet in the Armory.
Hastings also won the 55 dash last night, in 7.09 seconds, in leading Randolph to the girls team title with 120 points. Stuyvesant captured the boys title with 181 points.
