*Rick Milenthal, chief executive at HMS Partners, Columbus, Ohio, was named chairman at Ten United, the advertising agency with offices in Columbus, Ohio; Pittsburgh; and Orlando, Fla., which will now include HMS. Also, Nick Bandy, president at the Ten United Columbus office, becomes chief operating officer for all three offices, a new post. The appointments are part of a reorganization that eliminates an agency company, Ten Worldwide, under which Ten United and HMS operated.
