Meg Ryan, right, stars in ''Against the Ropes,'' a feminist-uplift boxing movie derived from the story of Jackie Kallen, one of the few women to succeed as a professional fight manager. Ms. Ryan, aided by a crusty trainer (Charles S. Dutton), navigates the career of a feisty prospect (Omar Epps) in the macho boxing world.
