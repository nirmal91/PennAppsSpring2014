*Culver Franchising System, Prairie du Sac, Wis., which has more than 240 Culver's quick-service restaurants in 12 states, parted ways with its agency of record since 2002, One and All Advertising, Minneapolis, and began a review for a new agency. Billings were estimated at $8 million to $10 million. Pile &amp; Company, Boston, will manage the review.
