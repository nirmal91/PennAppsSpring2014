The two-year reign of Fordham Prep of the Bronx as the Catholic High Schools Athletic Association boys' indoor champion ended yesterday at the New Balance Track and Field Center at the Armory in Upper Manhattan.
St. Anthony's of South Huntington outscored Fordham Prep, 28 1/3 to 28. Brian McCabe of Fordham Prep won the 1,600 meters (4 minutes 17.29 seconds) and the 3,200 (9:25.42).
Bishop Loughlin of Brooklyn won the girls' crown for the first time since 1985.
