The Department of Defense has identified 530 American service members who have died since the start of the Iraq war. It confirmed the death of the following Americans yesterday:
JIMENEZ, Linda C., 39, Sgt., Army; Brooklyn; Second Squadron Combat Support Aviation, Second Armored Cavalry.
RAMOS, Tamarra J., 24, Specialist, Army; Quakertown, Pa.; Third Armor Medical Company, Medical Troop Regimental Support Squadron, Third Armored Cavalry.
SCOTT, David A., 51, Master Sgt., Air Force; Union, Ohio; 445th Communications Flight.
SISUNG, David, 21, Petty Officer Third Class, Navy; Phoenix; U.S.S. Nimitz.
