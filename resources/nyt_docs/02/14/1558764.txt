To the Editor:
Re ''Seeking Memories of Bush at an Alabama Air Base'' (news article, Feb. 13):
Say what you will about the candidates for president, but service in the National Guard does not mean that one will not see action. The Virginia Air National Guard, for one, flew bombing missions over Iraq in the Persian Gulf war in 1991.
ALFRED BETHA  Charlottesville, Va., Feb. 13, 2004
