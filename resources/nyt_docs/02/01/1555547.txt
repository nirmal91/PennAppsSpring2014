Five weeks after Governor McGreevey endorsed Howard Dean, the former governor of Vermont, for the Democratic presidential nomination, Senator Jon S. Corzine chimed in last week with his choice.
Mr. Corzine, a leading Democratic fund-raiser and chairman of the Democratic Senatorial Campaign Committee, announced that he would back Senator John Kerry of Massachusetts.
Mr. Corzine cited Mr. Kerry's commitment to rebuilding international coalitions in fighting terrorism.
New Jersey's presidential primary will be held on June 8.
