To the Editor:
Re ''Greece's Colossal New Guilt Trip'' by Fred A. Bernstein [Jan. 18]:
Reviewing the 198 years since the ''acquisition'' of the Parthenon marbles by Lord Elgin, one has to conclude it was just plain theft. In light of this, should the headline have read, ''Britain's Colossal Old Guilt Trip''?
RIGOMAR A. THURMER  Boulder, Colo.
