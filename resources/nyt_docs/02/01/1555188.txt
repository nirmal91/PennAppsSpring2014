Your article raises issues of enhancement and fairness in sports. But academic test-taking may be the next high-performance arena to come under scrutiny. There are rumors of increased use on college and high-school campuses of drugs, specifically the medications to treat attention-deficit/hyperactivity disorder, like Ritalin or Adderall, during final exams or for national tests like the SAT.
Lawrence Diller, M.D.  Walnut Creek, Calif.
