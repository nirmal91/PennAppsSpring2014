After a 10-inch snowfall, New Yorkers and visitors choreographed a high-stepping snow dance. The star performers were candy-colored waterproof boots, above, and the occasional pair of what looked like polar bear paws. While most women, right, cheerfully adapted their fashion statement to the weather conditions, many men, left and below, stubbornly adhered to their rigid dress code, wearing their usual shoes.
