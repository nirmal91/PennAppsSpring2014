To the Editor:
Re ''Education Is No Protection,'' by Bob Herbert (column, Jan. 26):
There is no doubt about the sucking sound of white-collar jobs going abroad. But politicians in both parties, despite their vocal laments, won't or can't do anything about it.
Or can they?
Why not charge a corporate ''per person fee'' for each laid-off American, to pay for higher unemployment benefits, job training or something more creative?
BERT SHAW  Bearsville, N.Y., Jan. 28, 2004
