Natasha Hastings in the girls' 400 meters and the Camden High School boys' 4x200-meter relay team posted the best high school times indoors this season in the National Hall of Fame High School Classic yesterday.
Hastings, of A. P. Randolph in Manhattan, won the 400 in 54.12 seconds at the New Balance Track and Field Center at the Armory. Camden, pushed by a New Jersey rival, Vineland, won the relay in 1 minute 27.36 seconds, with Vineland a quarter of a second back.
Santia Moss of Pompano Beach, Fla., registered a national season-leading time in the girls' 55-meter hurdles, 8.00 seconds.
