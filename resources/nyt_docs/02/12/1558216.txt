The Department of Defense has identified 535 American service members who have died since the start of the Iraq war. It confirmed the death of the following Americans yesterday:
ROBBINS, Thomas D., 27, Sgt., Army; Schenectady, N.Y.; Troop A, First Squadron, 14th Cavalry.
WONG, Elijah Tai Wah, 42, Sgt., Army National Guard; Mesa, Ariz.; 363rd Explosive Ordnance Company.
