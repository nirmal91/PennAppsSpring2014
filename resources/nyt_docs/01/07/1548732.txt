The Department of Defense has identified 486 American service members who have died since the start of the Iraq war. It confirmed the death of the following Americans this week:
CORRAL, Dennis A., 33, Sgt., Army; Kearney, Neb.; First Infantry Division.
FRIST, Luke P., 20, Specialist, Army Reserve; West Lafayette, Ind.; 209th Quartermaster Company.
PALIWODA, Eric T., 23, Capt., Army; Texas; Fourth Infantry Division.
THE STRUGGLE FOR IRAQ  Correction:  January 14, 2004, Wednesday  A listing of American military deaths in Iraq on Jan. 7 misstated the age of Capt. Eric T. Paliwoda. He was 28, not 23.
