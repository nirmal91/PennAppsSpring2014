An 8-year-old girl died Saturday at a hospital in Hanoi, Vietnam,  from avian influenza, according to the World Health Organization and Vietnamese officials.
She was confirmed as the fifth human case of the disease; all have been fatal. The victims are believed to have become infected by some contact with chickens, but not from eating them or from eating eggs.
The girl became ill on Jan. 11 and was admitted to the hospital on Thursday. The W.H.O. said there was no evidence of person-to-person spread of the virus.
