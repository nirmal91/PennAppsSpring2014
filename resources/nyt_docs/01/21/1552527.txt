Because of an editing error, an article in Business Day yesterday about the opening of court proceedings in Germany against Josef Ackermann, chief executive of Deutsche Bank, referred incorrectly to a lawsuit brought by Leo Kirch, the fallen German media baron, against Mr. Ackermann's predecessor, Rolf E. Breuer. It contends that Mr. Breuer, not Mr. Kirch, conspired to destroy Mr. Kirch's media empire.
