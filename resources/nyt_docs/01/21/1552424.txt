The Department of Defense has identified 502 American service members who have died since the start of the Iraq war. It confirmed the death of the following Americans yesterday:
CASTRO, Roland L., 26, Staff Sgt., Army; San Antonio; First Battalion, 12th Field Artillery.
HORNBECK, Kelly L., 36, Master Sgt., Army; Fort Worth; Third Battalion, 10th Special Forces Group.
ORR, Cody J., 21, Pfc., Army; Ruskin, Fla.; Fourth Infantry Division.
POLLEY, Larry E. Jr., Specialist, Army; of Center, Tex.; Fourth Infantry Division.
RANDLE, Edmond L., 26, Sgt., Army; Miami; Fourth Infantry Division.
