Magazine advertising in 2003 ended on a downbeat as ad pages sank for a seventh consecutive month in December, according to information released yesterday by the Publishers Information Bureau.
Ad pages for all of 2003 fell 1 percent from 2002, the bureau reported.
For December, ad pages fell 4.6 percent compared with the month a year earlier as sales rose in 5 of 12 major categories. The largest declines came in beer, wine and liquor, down 22.4 percent.
