Unidentified men murdered a prominent Bangladeshi journalist in the country's turbulent southwest, Reuters reported. The reporter, Manik Shaha, 48, worked for the daily newspaper New Age and the BBC. Witnesses said several men stopped the rickshaw Mr. Shaha was riding in and hurled a bomb. Colleagues said he had been aggressively reporting on armed Maoist groups and criminal gangs operating in the area.
