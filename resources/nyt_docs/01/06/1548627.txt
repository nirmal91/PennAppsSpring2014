A bank teller at a savings bank branch in Queens foiled a robbery yesterday by refusing to hand over money, the police said. About noon, a man passed a note demanding money to the teller, at the Ridgewood Savings Ban branch at 205-11 Hillside Avenue, near Francis Lewis Boulevard. The man, identified by the police as Lawrence Thweet, 52, fled after failing to get any cash. He was caught by officers at the corner of 94th Avenue and 205th Street in Hollis, about three blocks from the bank, the police said. They charged him with attempted robbery.
