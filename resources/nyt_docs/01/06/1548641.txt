As the official vote count progressed slowly one day after the country's presidential election, it appeared that Mikhail Saakashvili was headed for an even more overwhelming win than the 86 percent projected by exit polls. With 25 percent of the vote counted, the Central Election Commission said he had taken 96.7 percent of the vote. The commission said about 83 percent of the 2.1 million eligible voters had taken part.
