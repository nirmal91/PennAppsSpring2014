A man was found dead in a car in Upper Manhattan yesterday, the police said. Officers responded to a call from the Hamilton Heights neighborhood at 1:41 p.m., the police said, and found the man, dead from a gunshot wound to the head, inside a red Oldsmobile that was parked outside 407 West 146th Street. The police said the man was in his 30's, but did not otherwise identify him.
