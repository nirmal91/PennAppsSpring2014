To the Editor:
It's only early January, and already David Brooks has made the understatement of the year! With nearly nine million unemployed, increased class size in schools and urgent needs of the environment being ignored, Mr. Brooks (column, Jan. 3) admits that President Bush's ''new domestic governing policy,'' compassionate conservatism, ''hasn't panned out.''
LOUISE S. MACDONALD  Ruxton, Md., Jan. 3, 2004
