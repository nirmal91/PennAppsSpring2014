Chuck Porter, chairman at Crispin Porter &amp; Bogusky, Miami, will also serve in a new post, chief strategist at MDC Partners, Toronto, which owns 49 percent of Crispin Porter.
Mike Fox, executive vice president and director for account management at Trone, High Point, N.C., was promoted to president.
Pat Pujolas was named creative director at Brokaw, Cleveland, succeeding Greg Thomas, who was promoted to general manager. Mr. Pujolas had been a writer for television and had worked at agencies like J. Walter Thompson and McCann-Erickson Worldwide Advertising.
Gianni Crespi was named president at Rodale International, London, a new division of Rodale combining Rodale Magazines International and Rodale Books International. Mr. Crespi had been managing director and general manager at Istituto Geografico De Agostini, Novara, Italy, working out of the Milan office. At Rodale, Mr. Crespi will divide time between London and a new Rodale International office in Milan.
