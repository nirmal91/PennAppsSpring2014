HIT Entertainment, London, expanded its relationship with Ziccardi Partners Frierson Mee, New York, by naming the agency to create worldwide marketing campaigns for videotapes and licensed merchandise related to HIT children's shows like ''Bob the Builder'' and ''Angelina Ballerina.'' HIT earlier hired Ziccardi to work on strategic development. Though spending has not been determined, HIT has spent $12 million to $15 million a year on marketing.
