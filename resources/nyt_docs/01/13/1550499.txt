The former mayor of Hainesport has pleaded guilty to diverting more than $300,000 in public funds for personal use, the Division of Criminal Justice said yesterday. Ronald Corn, who served as mayor from 1995 to 2001, has pleaded guilty to a charge of official misconduct. He faces a maximum sentence of 10 years in prison, $150,000 fine and restitution. Additionally, Mr. Corn will be barred from holding public office, the Division of Criminal Justice said.
