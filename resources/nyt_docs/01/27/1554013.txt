To the Editor:
Re ''The Bottom Line, a Place Where the Music Always Came First'' (Critic's Notebook, Jan. 26):
I don't think New York University quite grasps the damage the forced closing of the Bottom Line has done to the prestige and reputation of N.Y.U. within the community. It is as if N.Y.U. were metastasizing.
JEROME WEINBERGER  New York, Jan. 26, 2004
