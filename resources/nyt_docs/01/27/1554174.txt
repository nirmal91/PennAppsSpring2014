Gov. George E. Pataki appointed Peter R. Smith yesterday to serve as president of the New York State Energy Research and Development Authority, which is responsible for energy research. Mr. Smith, who has been the acting president for the past year, joined the agency in 1995, and became acting president on Feb. 5, 2003, succeeding William M. Flynn. As president, Mr. Smith oversees an authority with more than $330 million in assets.
Marc Santora (NYT)
