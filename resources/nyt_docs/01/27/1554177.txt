Three men pushed their way into a Bronx man's apartment yesterday and shot him in the face, the police said. The police, who did not release the man's identity, said the attack took place at 2:30 p.m. in an apartment at 41 Elliot Place in Mount Eden. The victim, who the police said was in his 20's, was taken to Lincoln Medical and Mental Health Center, where he died.
