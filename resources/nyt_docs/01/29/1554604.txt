The Dutch brewer Heineken said it was investing 28.5 million euros ($35.6 million) through an Asian-affiliated company to acquire a stake in Guangdong Brewery Holdings in southern China. The affiliate, Heineken Asia Pacific Breweries China, is acquiring 21 percent of Guangdong Brewery, whose subsidiaries make Kingway beer. A co-owner of the affiliate, Asia Pacific, is also investing 28.5 million euros. Guangdong Brewery will brew Heineken beer in addition to its own local product.
