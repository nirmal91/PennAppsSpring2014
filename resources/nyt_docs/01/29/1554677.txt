A Bronx man was arrested yesterday and charged with killing a 25-year-old man who died after being shot in the face by assailants who pushed their way into an apartment, the police said. The victim, identified as Roberto Quintana of Elliot Place in the Bronx, died at Lincoln Hospital on Tuesday. The police said the shot was fired as three men pushed their way into an apartment at 41 Elliot Place on Monday afternoon. Yesterday, the police arrested Johnny Torres, 29, and charged him with murder, robbery and criminal possession of a weapon. The other two men remain at large.
Stacy Albin (NYT)
