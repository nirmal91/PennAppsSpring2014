Addressing one of the persistent rumors surrounding the death of Diana, the princess of Wales, in a car accident in 1997, a former royal coroner said that the princess was not pregnant at the time of her death. The coroner, Dr. John Burton, who was present at the post-mortem of the princess, told The Evening Standard that he had spoken out in an effort to set the record straight because of the ''absurd'' conspiracy theories swirling around.
