Nina Planck, the director of Greenmarket, New York's system of farmers markets, was dismissed on Tuesday after 5 1/2 months on the job, said Lys McLaughlin, the executive director of the Council on the Environment of New York City, the organization that oversees Greenmarket. Ms. McLaughlin and Ms. Planck did not comment on the reason for the dismissal. Tom Strumolo, the former deputy director, has been named the new director.
Amanda Hesser (NYT)
