A company in Wisconsin has recalled 92,000 novelty candles because some resin casings have ignited, federal officials said. The manufacturer, Lang Candles of Delafield, announced the recall along with the Consumer Product Safety Commission. Lang Candles said it had received information about nine incidents, including six that resulted in property damage. The company said that refunds were available at (888) 526-4011.
