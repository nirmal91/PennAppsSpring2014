A Bronx man was fatally stabbed by a homeless man in a subway station yesterday, the police said. The two men were quarreling inside the 14th Street station of the Sixth Avenue subway line about 4:50 a.m., when the homeless man, Lloyd Koulen, stabbed the other man four times in the chin, the police said. The victim, John A. Wilson, 41, died in the station. Mr. Koulen, 28, was arrested and charged with second-degree murder, the police said.
