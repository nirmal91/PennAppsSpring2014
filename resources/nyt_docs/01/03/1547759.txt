An off-duty city police officer shot his roommate in the shoulder yesterday after the man threatened the officer with a bottle, the police said.
The roommate, Mandell Deck, 27, was arrested at 5:10 p.m. in the apartment in Jamaica, Queens, that he shared with the 37-year-old officer, who was not identified.
The police said Mr. Deck was intoxicated, and had tried to attack the officer with a bottle.
The officer then shot Mr. Deck, hitting him in the shoulder. Mr. Deck was taken to Jamaica Hospital Medical Center in stable condition.
He was charged with menacing and criminal possession of a weapon, the bottle.
