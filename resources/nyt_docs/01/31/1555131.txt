Playing its first game of the year, the United States women's team defeated Sweden, 3-0, yesterday in its opening match of the Four Nations tournament in Shenzhen, China. The United States got two goals from 20-year-old Lindsay Tarpley and one from Shannon Boxx. In the day's other match, China defeated Canada, 2-1. The United States was playing without Mia Hamm and Brandi Chastain, who are expected to join the team for regional Olympic qualifying matches next month in Costa Rica. The United States will play China tomorrow and complete play in the round-robin event against Canada on Tuesday.
