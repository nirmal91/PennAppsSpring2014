A fight in Queens yesterday left one young man stabbed to death and another in critical condition, the police said.
The two men, who the police said were in their teens or 20's, had a fight outside a building in Flushing at 5 p.m.
The police said that knives were used, and that one of the fighters died outside the building, at 136-42 Maple Avenue. The other young man was taken to New York Hospital Medical Center of Queens in critical condition, a police spokesman said. Officers detained seven men for questioning in connection with the fight, the spokesman said.
