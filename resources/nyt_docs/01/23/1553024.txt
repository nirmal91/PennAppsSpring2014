The electronics group Siemens said it earned 726 million euros ($923 million) in the first quarter, a rise of 39 percent from the period a year ago. Sales were down 3 percent, to 18.3 billion euros ($23.3 billion), but orders rose 2 percent, to 20.5 billion euros ($26 billion), Siemens said. The company, based in Munich, said sales of mobile phones helped operating profit at its mobile unit more than double.
