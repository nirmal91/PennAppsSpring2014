To the Editor:
Re ''The Big Test for the Contenders,'' by David Frum and Richard Perle (Op-Ed, Jan. 21):
The American people do recognize the danger that terrorism poses not only for our country, but throughout the world. Understanding the issues, and taking appropriate steps to deal with this scourge, are the only way our country and our way of life can remain safe.
