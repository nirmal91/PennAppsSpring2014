An Indian software exporter, Satyam Computer Services, said yesterday that it earned 1.46 billion rupees ($32 million) in its most recent quarter, up 25 percent from 1.17 billion rupees ($26 million) in the period a year ago. Satyam, based in Hyderabad, said it signed up 29 new customers and sales increased 33 percent in the period, which ended Dec. 31 and was the third quarter of its fiscal year.
