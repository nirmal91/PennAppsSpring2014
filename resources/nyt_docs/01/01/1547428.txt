TRANSOCEAN INC., Houston, the world's largest offshore oil driller, paid ConocoPhillips, Houston, the third-largest oil company in the United States, about $83 million for its 50 percent stake in their venture that drills in deep waters, gaining full ownership of the business.
I.B.M. CORP., Armonk, N.Y., said it had signed a 10-year contract with Target Corp., Minneapolis, the retailer, to manage its mainframe computers. Terms were not disclosed.
BRINK'S CO., Richmond, Va., the armored-car company, said it had sold its timber business to Forestland Group LLC, Chapel Hill, N.C., a timber company that manages about 1.2 million acres, for about $38 million.
