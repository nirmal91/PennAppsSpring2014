''Everything is very complicated,'' said Massimiliano Fuksas, an architect with studios in Rome, Paris and Vienna. ''I wanted to do something smaller and very clear.'' With his wife and partner, Doriana, he has created the brushed-chrome door lever with three holes, below, for Valli &amp; Valli, an Italian company. It is intended for a Ferrari research facility the couple are designing near Bologna.
