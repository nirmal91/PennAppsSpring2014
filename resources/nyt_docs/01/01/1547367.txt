The Department of Defense has identified 479 American service members who have died since the start of the Iraq war. It confirmed the death of the following Americans yesterday:
BLANCO, Ernesto M., 28, Capt., Army; Texas; 82nd Airborne Division.
SUTTER, Michael J., 28, Staff Sgt., Army National Guard; Tinley Park, Ill.; 745th Ordnance Company, 79th Ordnance Battalion.
