An article in Business Day on Jan. 13 about plans by Steven T. Florio to step down as chief executive of Condé Nast Publications misstated the timing of his successor's arrival there. The successor, Charles H. Townsend, headed the women's magazine division of The New York Times Company but joined Condé Nast in 1994, before -- not after -- that division was sold to Gruner &amp; Jahr USA Publishing.
