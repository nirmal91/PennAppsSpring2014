The W Hotels operate several lobby boutiques, but a new shop, on Lexington Avenue, is the first with a door from the street. ''We wanted to test if the store could become a destination,'' said Ross Klein, the company's chief marketing officer.
Will New Yorkers be lured inside to buy a $280 king-size Blue Maritime duvet cover, based largely on its association with the branch in San Diego? ''W is becoming a lifestyle authority,'' Mr. Klein said.
