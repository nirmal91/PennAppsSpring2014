The Reuters Group said Tuesday that it would cut its stake in Tibco Software by selling up to 60 million shares in a public offering, and another $115 million in Tibco stock back to the company. Reuters currently owns 103 million shares, or a 49 percent stake in Tibco. Tibco, based in Palo Alto, Calif., makes programs that connect businesses to one another.
