For the record, I'm not a zealot or a tree-hugging ''Naderite'' (Russell Shorto, Jan. 11). What I am is an average American who is slowly waking up to find that the most critical aspects of our food supply have been turned over to the same interests who brought us Love Canal and the exploding Pinto.
I'm ready to pay $3.50 for Tod Murphy's grilled cheese sandwich.
Bill Daunch  Cary, N.C.
