Drapery Encores, a consignment shop in New Jersey, sells slightly used window treatments, mainly from show houses and designer showrooms. The styles, including formal drapes and decorative shades, are in patterns like toile de Jouy, striped silks, damasks and French Provençal prints.
This week, drapes in sheer bronze Scalamandré silk costing $4,000 new are $680. And a yellow cotton balloon shade with Osborne &amp; Little fringe, $1,100 new, is $300. At 1275 Bloomfield Avenue in Fairfield; www.draperyencores or (973) 227-9222 (closed Sunday and Monday).
