The Coca-Cola Company, Atlanta, expanded its relationship with Grey Worldwide in Atlanta, part of the Grey Global Group, by naming it to create advertising to introduce Diet Coke with Lime. Billings were estimated at $2 million to $5 million. The Atlanta office of Grey Worldwide will create radio and outdoor ads; Coca-Cola is handling online advertising internally using work produced by Grey. Foote Cone &amp; Belding, New York, part of the Interpublic Group of Companies, is creative agency for the Diet Coke brand.
