Bobby Jindal, a Republican who lost a runoff election for governor in November, has announced that he will seek the Congressional seat being vacated by Representative David Vitter, a fellow Republican who is running for the Senate. Mr. Jindal, 32, was ahead in the polls for much of the race for governor but lost to Kathleen Blanco, a Democrat, 48 percent to 52 percent.
Ariel Hart (NYT)
