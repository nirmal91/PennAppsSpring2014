An investment group led by Hicks, Muse, Tate &amp; Furst, a leveraged buyout firm based in Dallas, has agreed to buy Persona, a Canadian cable operator and Internet service provider, for $307 million. Persona, based in St. John's, Newfoundland, with operations in seven provinces, will be taken private. The buyer group includes the private equity arms of the Toronto-Dominion Bank and the Canadian Imperial Bank of Commerce. Persona will spin off its operations in the Bahamas.
