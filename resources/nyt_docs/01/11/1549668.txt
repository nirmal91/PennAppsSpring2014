To the Editor:
In his review of Rick Bragg's ''I Am a Soldier, Too'' (Dec. 14), David Lipsky says that Jessica Lynch's return to the United States topped ''a Frank Capra movie.'' It is a good analogy, except that he should have said ''a Preston Sturges movie'' (''Hail the Conquering Hero'').
Paul R. Waldman  Tarrytown, N.Y.
