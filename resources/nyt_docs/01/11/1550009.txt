To the Editor:
Re ''Second Thoughts on Free Trade,'' by Charles Schumer and Paul Craig Roberts (Op-Ed, Jan. 6):
After American companies succeed in outsourcing many of their jobs in the name of cutting costs and in the process create large numbers of unemployed or minimally employed in America, to whom in the ''home markets'' will these companies sell their products?
ELIZABETH BURKE  Murray Hill, N.J., Jan. 7, 2004
