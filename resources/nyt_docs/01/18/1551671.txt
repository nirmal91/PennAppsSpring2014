NEW YORK
GOVERNMENT OFFICES -- Federal, state and city offices closed.  POST OFFICE -- Express mail delivery only. The main post office at 33rd Street and Eighth Avenue will be open.  BANKS -- Option to close.  PARKING -- Alternate-side parking regulations will be suspended. Parking meter rules will be in effect.  SANITATION -- Regular garbage pickups and recycling collection. Street cleaning will be suspended.  SCHOOLS -- Closed.  FINANCIAL MARKETS -- Stock and bond markets will be closed.  TRANSPORTATION -- New York City subways and buses will operate on a regular schedule. L.I.R.R. will operate on a modified weekday schedule, running fewer trains than on a regular weekday. Metro North will run on a Saturday schedule, with additional trains at rush hour.
NEW JERSEY
GOVERNMENT OFFICES -- Federal and state offices closed.  POST OFFICES -- Express mail delivery only.  BANKS -- Option to close.  SCHOOLS -- Option to close.  TRANSPORTATION -- N.J. Transit trains and buses and PATH trains will run on regular weekday schedules.
CONNECTICUT
GOVERNMENT OFFICES -- Federal and state offices closed.  POST OFFICES -- Express mail delivery only.  BANKS -- Option to close.  SCHOOLS -- Closed.  TRANSPORTATION -- Metro North will run on a Saturday schedule, with additional trains at rush hour.
