North Carolina A&amp;T, a Mid-Eastern Athletic Conference university located in Greensboro, is the only remaining Division I men's basketball team without a victory this season.
The Aggies lost, 64-42, to Coppin State last night in Baltimore to fall to 0-14 over all and 0-5 in the conference. They were 1-26 last season.
Campbell was the only other program without a victory entering yesterday's games, but the Camels defeated Jacksonville, 85-73, in Buies Creek, N.C., to improve to 1-13 and 1-6 in the Atlantic Sun Conference.
Campbell's victory was the first for Robbie Laing as a Division I head coach.
