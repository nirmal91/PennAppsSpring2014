Clive Sirkin, executive vice president and account director on the Kellogg account at Leo Burnett Worldwide, Chicago, part of the Publicis Groupe, was named to new duties for the agency, as group managing director. He will now also oversee worldwide business strategy and operations for multinational clients as well as oversee the Burnett offices in Canada and continue to lead the Kellogg account.
David Jones, global brand director on the Reckitt Benckiser account at Euro RSCG Worldwide, part of Havas, was named to added duties for the agency, as executive vice president and managing director for global business. He remains based in London.
Rob Gregory was named group publisher at Maxim magazine, New York, part of Dennis Publishing, succeeding Jamie Hooper, who is leaving to help start a family business, Dennis said. Mr. Gregory had been publisher at Rolling Stone magazine, New York, owned by Wenner Media.
