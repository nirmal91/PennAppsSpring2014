Advertising revenue for radio networks and stations continued to fall in November, the Radio Advertising Bureau in New York reported.
Combined local and national ad revenue declined 4 percent in November, the bureau said, compared with a year ago. The decrease came after a 1 percent decline in October compared with October 2002.
For the first 11 months of 2003, combined ad revenue rose 1 percent compared with the year-earlier period, the bureau said.
