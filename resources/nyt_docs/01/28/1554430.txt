A security guard charged with raping a woman in a Brownsville homeless shelter on Sunday was arraigned yesterday and suspended without pay by the Department of Homeless Services, city officials said. The guard, Jason Williams, 27, was charged with first-degree rape, third-degree rape and sexual misconduct. A police spokesman said that Mr. Williams followed a 27-year-old woman into the bathroom of a shelter on Williams Avenue at 3 a.m. and raped her. The police have said Mr. Williams told them that the woman consented.
