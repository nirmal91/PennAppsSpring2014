New York's largest union of health care workers, 1199/S.E.I.U., announced yesterday that home health aides at Best Care Inc., one of the city's largest providers of home care services to the ill and elderly, had voted overwhelmingly to join 1199. Union officials said that during a two-week mail-in vote that ended last week, 1,227 health aides had voted to join the union and 233 had voted against.
Steven Greenhouse (NYT)
