German business confidence climbed for the ninth consecutive month, according to a survey of 7,000 companies in western German, which was released by the Ifo Institute in Munich. The overall index rose to 97.4 from 96.9 in December, the highest reading for three years. Sentiment was strongest in the manufacturing and construction sectors, while retail and wholesale companies, became more pessimistic during the month, the institute said.
