The Coors Brewing division of the Adolph Coors Company in Golden, Colo., has signed the first major sponsorship deal for ''Jimmy Kimmel Live,'' the late-night comedy-variety show by the ABC television division of the Walt Disney Company. Terms of the deal, to be announced today, were not disclosed.
As part of the deal, Coors Light beer will sponsor music segments on the Kimmel show throughout 2004, averaging about one segment a week, under the title of ''The 'Jimmy Kimmel Live' Concert Series.'' The first segment, with the singer Enrique Iglesias, is scheduled for tonight.
Coors Light will also sponsor streaming video of the segments on the ABC Web site (abc.com).
