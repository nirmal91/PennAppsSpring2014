A woman and her 2-year-old daughter died in a fire last night in New Haven a spokesman for the New Haven Police Department said.
The woman, who was 24, and her daughter, were found in a third floor apartment of a multiple-family house at 175 Plymouth Street, where the fire began at 6:15 p.m., according to the police spokesman, Sgt. Ray Johannes said.
Firefighters fought the blaze for more than an hour. Sergeant Johannes did not identify the victims. Deputy Fire Chief William Ryan said the fire did not spread beyond the third floor, where it began, but it did leave a number of people homeless.
