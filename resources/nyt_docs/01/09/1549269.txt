Three members of the defending Stanley Cup champion Devils -- goaltender Martin Brodeur and defensemen Scott Stevens and Scott Niedermayer -- were named yesterday as starters for the Eastern Conference team in next month's National Hockey League All-Star Game. They received the most votes at their positions in fan balloting.
The forwards voted to the Eastern team were Ilya Kovalchuk of Atlanta, Martin St. Louis of Tampa Bay and Joe Thornton of Boston.
Voted as starters for the Western Conference were goaltender Marty Turco and forwards Bill Guerin and Mike Modano, all of Dallas; forward Todd Bertuzzi of Vancouver; and defensemen Nicklas Lidstrom of Detroit and Rob Blake of Colorado.
The game is Feb. 8 in St. Paul.
