Executives from two agencies in Minneapolis, Fallon Worldwide and Carmichael Lynch, have left to start an agency there called Mono, which opens with Sesame Workshop as its first client.
The executives are Jim Scott, managing partner, and Michael Hart and Chris Lange, creative directors. Mr. Scott, 36, had been an account director at Carmichael Lynch, part of the Interpublic Group of Companies. Mr. Hart, 36, and Mr. Lange, 32, had been group creative directors at Fallon, part of the Publicis Groupe.
Mono's first client, Sesame Workshop in New York, is the producer of ''Sesame Street'' and other children's TV programming. Sesame Workshop had worked with various agencies on a project basis. Billings have not been determined.
