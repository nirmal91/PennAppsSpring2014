To the Editor:
Re ''Niagara's Trickle-Down Theory: Waiting to Share a Casino's Success'' (news article, Jan. 3):
How incredibly sad. Niagara Falls, N.Y., the former ''King of Power, Queen of Beauty,'' reduced to groveling for scraps from gambling tables! A modern fable with a prophetic ending.
EVELYN C. GRANIERI  New York, Jan. 6, 2004
