Chris Wingert, a defender for St. John's, was named the winner of the Hermann Trophy last night in St. Louis. The trophy is awarded each year by the Missouri Athletic Club to the nation's top male and female collegiate soccer players.
Wingert helped St. John's reach the final of of the N.C.A.A. men's Division I soccer championship last month in Columbus, Ohio, where the Red Storm lost to Indiana, 2-1.
Wingert, 21, was also named the scholar-athlete of the year by the National Soccer Association of America on Dec. 23. He is the first person to win both awards.
